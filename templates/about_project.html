Homify 

This is my cs50's final project. CS50 is a Computer Science course (on-campus and online) offered by the Harvard University. cs50 is quite popular among CS courses. My project name is Homify, where you can search for rental homes and homestays. Homify also gives flexibility to property owners to list their properties for rent. Homify is licensed under the MIT license. This project is developed using Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Flask, and Bootstrap framework (version 4.5 and 5) for grids and containers. As per Github's estimation, Homify uses 60.2% of HTML, 17.7% of Python (1424 lines), 13.3% of CSS, and 8.8% of JavaScript.
Here are many of the resources that I've used for the development of this project:
  • Color palettes:   Coolors
  • Icons:   Font Awesome  and  Boxicon
  • Photos and SVGs:   Unsplash,  Pexels, and  Undraw
  • Quick study sites:   MDN Web Docs,  W3 schools ,  Geek for Geeks, and  of course Google search
